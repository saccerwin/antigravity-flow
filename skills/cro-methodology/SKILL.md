# CRO Methodology

## When to Use
CRO audit: funnel mapping → persuasion assets → objection handling → A/B test design.

## Procedure
1. Understand the audience, channel, and conversion goal.
2. Research or generate the necessary assets.
3. Audit the result for clarity, consistency, and measurable impact.

## Expected Output
- Research-backed recommendations
- Ready-to-use assets or tracking setup
- Measurement and iteration notes

Map funnel, identify objections, design experiments, and measure conversion impact.
