# codexkit-customer-support

Use this skill for support operations work: triaging incoming cases, determining severity, building escalation packets for engineering, and drafting customer-facing updates. Keep outputs concrete, empathy-aware, and operationally useful.

## Capabilities

- support-triage
- severity-framing
- customer-response-shaping




## Expected Output

- Summarize issue, impact, evidence, and next owner in a support-usable format.
- Differentiate acknowledgment, workaround, escalation, and final resolution states.
- Keep customer language calm, clear, and specific.
