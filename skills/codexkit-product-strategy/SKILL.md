# codexkit-product-strategy

Use this skill for product management and strategy tasks such as scoping features, turning ideas into executable briefs, and aligning stakeholders around scope, risks, and delivery expectations. Prefer crisp definitions over aspirational product language.

## Capabilities

- feature-framing
- tradeoff-writing
- stakeholder-alignment




## Expected Output

- Clarify problem, target user, success metric, scope, and open risks.
- Separate assumptions from confirmed requirements.
- Surface tradeoffs and rollout concerns before execution starts.
