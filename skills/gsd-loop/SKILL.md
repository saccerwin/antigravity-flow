# codexkit-get-shit-done

Use a get-shit-done style loop when momentum matters more than heavy process. Convert goals into a short execution ladder, keep blockers visible, and verify after each slice.

## Operating Notes
- Break work into concrete next actions with explicit done conditions.
- Keep the active slice small enough to verify quickly.
- Surface blockers immediately and convert them into tracked follow-up work.
- End each pass with status, next action, and residual risk.
