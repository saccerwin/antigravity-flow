# CodexKit iOS Analytics

Use this skill for product and technical instrumentation work.

## Workflow
1. Define screen events, CTA events, and success metrics before coding.
2. Keep naming stable and payload contracts explicit.
3. Verify deduplication across appear, refresh, retry, and navigation paths.
4. Check privacy, ATT, and App Store review exposure.
5. Validate the emitted signals through realistic flows.
6. Record downstream reporting assumptions when known.
